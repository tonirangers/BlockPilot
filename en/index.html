Why 1 client = 1 Safe
  • Isolated risk: no commingling with other clients.
  • You stay owner/signer: we can’t withdraw without you.
  • Auditable: everything is verifiable (Zeal / DeBank / Etherscan).
  • Simple withdrawals: you sign, it moves.
