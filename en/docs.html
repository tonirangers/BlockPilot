CHAPTER 4: SECURITY & WITHDRAWALS

4.1 The “Glass” (The Guard)

The Guard is a security filter. It authorizes BlockPilot to interact with financial protocols, but technically blocks simple transfers to unknown addresses.

4.2 The “Hammer” (Emergency Kit)

If BlockPilot disappears, you are not locked out. You have a technical emergency kit to "break the glass" (revoke our rights) and recover your funds yourself.

4.3 Withdrawals: How it works

The process is designed to prevent theft:

  1. You send an email with the amount and destination address (must be whitelisted).
  2. We disengage funds from protocols (a few hours to 48h depending on liquidity).
  3. We execute the transfer.
  • Flexibility: Partial or total withdrawal possible at any time.
  • Network Fees (Gas): BlockPilot covers internal management fees. Fees related to a specific withdrawal may depend on the network and are governed by the T&Cs.
  • Address Addition: Adding a new address requires a mandatory 24-hour security delay.

CHAPTER 5: YIELD & PERFORMANCE

5.1 Understanding Yield

DeFi allows putting your capital to work via on-chain markets (Lending, Liquidity), with rates varying based on supply and demand.

Our goal: competitive net yield, with prudent management (diversification, exposure caps). Yield is not guaranteed and may vary.

HODL (Dormant) Your crypto sleeps.
0% yield.
BLOCKPILOT (Active) Yield + Farming.
Compound interest.

5.2 Farming & Incentives (The Bonus)

In addition to standard interest, some protocols distribute rewards (tokens) to attract liquidity.

Our strength: We capture these bonuses. We regularly sell them to lock in profit in Stablecoins, then reinvest them into your capital. This is the magic of compound interest.

If rewards are illiquid, they are tracked separately as an optional "upside".
