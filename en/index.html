Segregated Assets

Your capital is held in a dedicated vault in your name. It is never commingled with BlockPilot funds or other clients.

Double Validation

Maximum security: any withdrawal requires your electronic signature. We cannot move funds alone.

Total Liquidity

No lock-up. You can recover your funds at any time, without our permission (Sovereignty).

Zero Counterparty Risk
  • Unlike a bank or exchange (FTX), you are not depositing money with us.
  • You grant us a technical management mandate.
  • If BlockPilot disappears tomorrow, your assets remain in your vault, accessible by you alone.
Governance & Controls
  • Whitelisting: Withdrawals restricted to your own bank/crypto accounts only.
