8.1 The BlockPilot Dictionary (Glossary)

  • DeFi (Decentralized Finance): Open financial system operating on the Blockchain, without classic banking intermediaries, allowing to generate returns (Yield).
  • Gas (Network Fees): The "fuel" paid to validate a transaction on the Blockchain. These fees go to network validators, not to BlockPilot.
  • Guard: Security module installed on your Safe that technically restricts outgoing transactions to prevent theft.
  • IBAN: Your standard bank account number, provided via the Zeal application to deposit Euros.
  • KYC (Know Your Customer): Identity verification procedure (ID Card + Selfie) requested by banking partners (Zeal/MEXC) to activate Fiat deposits.
  • Safe (Smart Wallet): Your personal digital vault. Unlike a simple wallet, it allows advanced security rules (multisignature).
  • Seed Phrase (Recovery Phrase): The sequence of 12 or 24 words that constitutes the master key of your wallet. Never to be shared.
  • Stablecoin (USDC, USDT, EURe): A cryptocurrency whose value is pegged to a real currency (1 USDC = 1 Dollar). It does not suffer from Bitcoin volatility.
  • Yield: Interest generated by your capital when placed in financial protocols (Lending, Liquidity).
